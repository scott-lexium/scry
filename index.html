Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

A full-featured Android desktop companion. No terminal, no manual ADB setup, no fumbling with flags.

Auto-install ADB & scrcpy
No setup required. The app downloads and installs ADB and scrcpy to the standard system locations automatically.
One-click WiFi mirroring
Plug in once over USB, click Switch to WiFi, and go wireless. The app handles the ADB TCP/IP setup automatically.
Presets & profiles
Built-in presets for Low Latency, High Quality, Screencast, and Gaming. Save and switch custom profiles in one click.
Quick device controls
Home, Back, Recents, Volume, Screenshot, Lock — all one click while mirroring. No need to touch the phone.
APK sideloader
Drag and drop any APK onto the app window to install it instantly. No commands, no Android Studio needed.
Built-in ADB terminal & Logcat
Run shell commands and stream device logs directly inside the app. Filter by tag and level with colour-coded output.
Setup

Up and running in minutes

No technical knowledge required. The app walks you through every step.

1
Download and open scrcpy GUI
Download the installer for your platform, open it, and drag the app to your Applications folder. On first launch the app checks for ADB and scrcpy automatically.
2
Let the app install ADB and scrcpy for you
If ADB or scrcpy are not detected, the Setup panel opens automatically. Click Auto Install — the app downloads the official binaries, extracts them to the standard system locations, and adds them to your PATH. No terminal required.
~/Library/Android/sdk/platform-tools/adb
3
Plug in your phone and tap Allow
Connect your Android phone via USB. Enable USB debugging in Developer Options, then tap Allow USB debugging on the dialog that appears on your phone screen. The app detects it instantly and prompts you.
4
Hit Launch
Pick a preset or configure your settings, then click Launch scrcpy. Your phone screen appears in a window on your desktop. You're done.
Security

You may see a security warning

macOS and Windows warn about apps that aren't signed by a paid developer certificate. scrcpy GUI is completely safe — here's how to proceed on each platform.

🍎 macOS Gatekeeper
"scrcpy GUI cannot be opened because it is from an unidentified developer."
  1. Find the app in Finder (not Launchpad)
  2. Right-click the app → Open
  3. Click Open in the dialog that appears
  4. The app opens normally from now on
🪟 Windows SmartScreen
"Windows protected your PC — Microsoft Defender SmartScreen prevented an unrecognized app from starting."
  1. Click More info in the blue dialog
  2. Click Run anyway
  3. The installer proceeds normally
🐧 Linux AppImage
The AppImage may not be executable by default after download.
  1. Open a terminal in the download folder
  2. Run chmod +x scrcpy-gui-*.AppImage
  3. Double-click the file to launch, or run ./scrcpy-gui-*.AppImage
